Kia ora, I'm Rebecca. I'm from a small town called Ashhurst but I now live in Wellington and have called Wellington home for the past 10 years.

I value humour, adventure, honesty and peace the most at this stage in my life. I am always on the hunt for my next adventure, whether that is signing up for my next run event or putting my hand up to do something different in the weekend - anything can be an adventure, it's all about mindset.

I decided to study with Dev Academy this year as I'm at a crossroads in my career - I could continue being a SDA, which I have been doing for five years, or I could upskill and move into a job that is interesting and challenges me.

I've always been creative, whether I'm writing a blog about my travels or coming up with app ideas, it seems like the path that I'm on now will give me the tools I need to meet my creative goals.

Outside of study/work, I enjoy spending time with my husband and family - sign me up for a board game night anytime. I spend a good chunk of my spare time exercising. I ran my first half marathon last year, and I'm hoping to run another later this year. When I'm not running or playing Catan, I can be found cooking something delicious in the kitchen, or having a laugh on the phone with my mum or simply dreaming up my next adventure.
